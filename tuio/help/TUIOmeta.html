A meta TUIO.

Decide which classID cover which sort of TUIO by setting 

Meta_TUIOmeta:tuioClasses



Example


TUIOmeta.tuioClasses = [Club, JugglersHead];


t = TUIOServer("_ixyzuvw", TUIOmeta);

t.set(0, [0, 0.3, 0.7, 0.5, 0.2, 0.4, 0.1])

t.visibleObjs

t.set(0, [1, 0.3, 0.7, 0.5, 0.2, 0.4, 0.1])

t.visibleObjs

t.gui
